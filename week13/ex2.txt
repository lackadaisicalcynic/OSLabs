Recovery is the way to get rid of deadlock when it is already happened by backing up to the safe states. However, it might work 
  for a long time going back and forward.
Avoidance requires all needed information about resourses usage.
Prevention is set of rules we follow to avoid the deadlock. We assure that at least one of conditions: Mutual-Exclusion, 
  hold-and-wait, no-preeemption, cirular wait is never satisfied. However it might not be always possible to follow those rules.
